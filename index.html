Welcome to Capture The King!

Capture The King is a chess game variant. The game is a lot more agressive than your typical match of chess, because the goal is to capture the opponents king, not trap it. For that reason the games are on average more fast-paced. There is no indications for checks in this game, You will have to rely on your decision making skills.
